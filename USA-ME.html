USA flight activities tracked in Middle east airspace + found(1) UK airforce activity in Cyprus

By Ved S| Firewolf600 June 24, 2025 4 min read

Couple hours after the US bombed Iran a possible Lockheed C-130 Hercules with no callsign was seen , it most likely departed from King Abdulaziz Air Base (DHA/OEDR) a known Saudi Royal Air Force installation near Dhahran.

The C-130 Hercules is known for its versitile role from being a gunship to a cargo hold or an refueler/surveillance so it is not possible to speculate any critical info from this flight, nonetheless it was fun tracking this plane
