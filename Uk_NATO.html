A high concentration of UK military aircraft has been recorded, possibly linked to the NATO summit

By Ved S | Firewolf600 June 24, 2025 2 min read

Several(3) UK Royal Airforce unis where spotted in UK airspace during the NATO summit,2 of them flying side by side probably toward Amsterdam where the summit was taking place or flying around airspaces to maintain survelance, GUNFTR22 & GUNFTR21

The third unit was Observed flying over the waters alongside the coastline of UK, WARLOR31
